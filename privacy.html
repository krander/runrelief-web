Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Overview

RunRelief ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help runners find nearby public bathrooms during training runs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What Data We Collect

Location Data

RunRelief requires access to your device's location while the app is in use. We use your location to:

  • Find public bathrooms near you
  • Display your position on the map
  • Auto-fill your location when submitting a community bathroom pin

We only access your location while the app is open and in use. We do not track your location in the background.

Anonymous Device Identifier

When you first open RunRelief, we generate a random anonymous identifier (UUID) and store it on your device. This identifier:

  • Is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information
  • Is used solely to associate community bathroom submissions with a device
  • Cannot be used to identify you personally

Community Submitted Pins

If you choose to report a bathroom location, we store the following in our database:

  • Latitude and longitude of the reported location
  • Type of bathroom (porta-potty or restroom)
  • Your anonymous device identifier
  • Submission timestamp
  • Expiry date (pins auto-expire after 30 days)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.

What Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email, or account information
  • No photos or camera data
  • No contacts or calendar data
  • No payment information
  • No browsing history
  • No data from other apps

How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above solely to:

  • Show you nearby bathroom locations
  • Display community-reported pins on the map
  • Allow other runners to benefit from your community submissions

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond core app functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

RunRelief uses the following third-party services to operate:

Supabase
We use Supabase to store community-submitted bathroom pins. Supabase is a secure cloud database provider. Community pin data (location coordinates, type, anonymous identifier) is stored on Supabase servers. You can review Supabase's privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy.

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API
Permanent public bathroom locations are sourced from OpenStreetMap, an open-source mapping project. We query the Overpass API using your approximate location to find nearby bathrooms. No personal data is sent to OpenStreetMap. You can review OpenStreetMap's privacy policy at https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy.

Apple Maps
When you tap "Take Me There," RunRelief opens Apple Maps on your device for walking directions. This interaction is governed by Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy.

Data Retention

  • Community pins automatically expire and are deleted after 30 days
  • Flagged pins are marked as inactive and excluded from results
  • Anonymous device identifiers are stored on your device and in our database only in association with your submitted pins

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any community pins associated with your anonymous device identifier by contacting us at the email address below. Since we do not collect personal information, we cannot identify you by name or email — please include your anonymous device identifier if possible (accessible in app settings).

Children's Privacy

RunRelief is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
